How to Register

Joining the library is easy and free! Visit the library with the required documents and fill out a registration form.

Documents Required

  • Valid Government-issued ID (Aadhar Card, Passport, etc.)
  • Recent Passport-size Photograph
  • Proof of Address (Utility bill, rental agreement, etc.)
